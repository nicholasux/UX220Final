What is the student success community hub?

It is a community run website that aims to offer students a better way to succeed in their courses.

About

The Student Success Community Gub was created by students that wanted to improve the learning experience for Laurier students.

We strive to create a free community website where students can receive additional material and learn with peers by engaging in community discussions. The course materials will primarily be created by students that have already succeeded in the course.

Contact

If you need any additional assistance, feel free to contact us at (123)-123-1234 or at admin@studentsuccess.com.

We are open weekdays from 9am - 5pm and will respond to emails within 2 business days
